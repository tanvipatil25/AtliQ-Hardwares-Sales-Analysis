# AtliQ Hardwares Sales Performance Analysis

## Project Overview
This project involves analyzing the global sales performance of AtliQ Hardwares, a leading computer hardware manufacturer. The objective of this analysis is to evaluate market performance against predetermined targets and track customer net sales growth from 2019 to 2021 to support data-driven decision-making.

## Key Reports Generated
1. **Market Performance vs. Target:** * A country-wise analysis comparing actual sales in 2021 against target values.
   * Highlights percentage variances to quickly identify overperforming and underperforming regions (e.g., evaluating the -$10.2M variance in the USA).
2. **Customer Net Sales Performance:** * A detailed breakdown of net sales by customer/retailer (e.g., Amazon, BestBuy, Croma, AtliQ Exclusive).
   * Tracks year-over-year data (2019, 2020, 2021) and highlights the massive percentage growth from 2020 to 2021 for key clients.

## Tools & Technologies Used
* **Microsoft Excel:** Data cleaning, transformation, and analysis.
* **Pivot Tables & Power Pivot:** For aggregating massive datasets and creating dynamic views.
* **Conditional Formatting:** To visually highlight negative variances and strong growth metrics.

## Key Insights
* Tracked the company's overall revenue growth, reaching a grand total of **$598.9M** in net sales in 2021.
* Identified key global markets that fell short of 2021 targets, providing actionable insights for the regional sales teams to adjust their strategies.
* Highlighted exponential growth in specific retail partnerships (e.g., Amazon and AtliQ e Store) recovering from 2020 to 2021.
